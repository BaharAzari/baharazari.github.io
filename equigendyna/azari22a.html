Abstract

Learning representations through deep generative modeling is a powerful approach for dynamical modeling to discover the most simplified and compressed underlying description of the data, to then use it for other tasks such as prediction. Most learning tasks have intrinsic symmetries. We propose an SO(3) equivariant deep dynamical model (EqDDM) for motion prediction that learns a structured representation whose embedding varies with symmetry transformations. EqDDM uses equivariant networks to parameterize state-space emission and transition models, and demonstrates superior predictive performance on various motion data.
